Using Quotations in HTML

When you need to quote a short phrase, you can use the <q> tag. For example, he said, Life is what happens when you're busy making other plans. It automatically adds quotation marks.

For longer, block-level quotations, the <blockquote> tag is more appropriate:

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) is the main international standards organization for the World Wide Web. It is responsible for developing protocols & guidelines that ensure the long-term growth of the Web.
