URI Encoding Example

A URL cannot contain spaces or certain special characters like '&'. They must be "percent-encoded".

Incorrect Link (with special characters):
https://example.com/search?query=HTML & CSS

Correct Encoded Link:
https://example.com/search?query=HTML%20%26%20CSS

In the corrected link, the space is encoded as %20 and the ampersand is encoded as %26.
